Participation in the Xano Community requires following the [community-code-of-conduct.md](community-code-of-conduct.md "mention") and the [community-content-modification-policy.md](community-content-modification-policy.md "mention")

## Email Support

Email support is available to users on our Starter plan and above. You can reach out to our support team by emailing support@xano.com, and we will respond as soon as we can.

It's important to ensure that as much information as possible is provided in your initial message to ensure a timely and helpful response, listed below.

## Live Chat Support

Live chat support is available for users on our Pro plans and above, from 2AM to 6PM PST during regular business days, typically Monday - Friday, except for holidays.

You can access our support chat by clicking  :ring\_buoy: <mark style="background-color:blue;">Help</mark> in the lower-left corner anywhere inside of Xano, and clicking <mark style="background-color:blue;">Support Chat</mark>&#x20;

## Office Hours

Once a week, we hold live Office Hours sessions with members of the Xano team and community. These sessions give you an opportunity to present an issue or question you might have about working in Xano and get limited live assistance.

These sessions are free, and available to everyone. You do need to sign up in advance, which can be done by visiting [this link.](https://community.xano.com/events)

## Premium Support

Premium Support is available as an add-on for our **Pro** plan. You can add this at any time from your Billing screen.

* **Cost:** $150/mo
* **Business Day SLA**: 8 hr response time

## White House Support

Users on a custom plan can opt to include White House support, enabling a special escalation path for critical issues.

This escalation service is limited to addressing service outages, not standard support issues. If minimizing downtime is critical for your business, this service is ideal.

During a service outage, you can page the on-call engineer, with typical response times under an hour.

**Common causes of system instability include:**

* Increased traffic overloading the system
* Increase in data volume stressing the database
* Denial of service attacks
* User errors within the function stack
* Hardware problems

**Possible courses of action include:**

* Issue diagnosis
* Temporary increase in server resources
* Database index optimization for performance
* Rate limiting assistance for denial of service attacks
* Long-term recommendations for functionality changes or plan upgrades to enhance protection against attacks and increased traffic

To initiate a White House escalation, open the support chat and **start a new conversation**. Two options will appear: normal premium support or emergency support - **select the emergency support option**.

{% hint style="info" %}
This option will only appear if the White House support service is enabled on your plan.
{% endhint %}

This method must be used in order for auditing purposes and emergency alert notifications to reach the Xano engineer on call. The use of other channels cannot guarantee the outlined response times.&#x20;
